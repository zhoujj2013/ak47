1.Category=GO or PATHWAY category
2.Count=gene number assigned to this category
3.%=percentage of gene assigned to this category
4.Pvalue=p-value from hypergeometric test
5.List Total=gene number assigned to all categories
6.Pop Hits=all gene number assigned to this category in specific species
7.Pop Total=all gene number assigned to all categories in specific species
8.Fold Enrichment=Fold enrichment in gene number
9.Bonferroni=Bonferroni adjusted p-value
10.Benjamini=Benjamini adjusted p-value
11.FDR=False discovery rate
12.Term=Category description
13.Genes=Gene list assigned to this category
